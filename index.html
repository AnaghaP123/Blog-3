My Favorite Movies

The Parent Trap
A great family movie

The Parent Trap is an amazing movie about twins that were separated at birth. They each had completely different upbringings but by a coincidence they met at a summer camp.

Annie
A story that was told many times

Annie is the story of an orphan whose name was by everyone's surprise Annie. Annie grew up in an orphanage and ran away many times, but during her punishment for running away the assistant to a rich person came lookin for an orphan. From there, Annie was then adopted by the rich person, but not before the owner of the orphanage and her brother make a plot to steal money from her. There are many retellings of this story since the first movie was made.
